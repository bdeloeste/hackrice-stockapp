Our dream of the ideal stock market:

The values of companies are decided not by complicated economic forces that no one really understands but by how many people companies can make happy

Only companies that people care about are publicly traded

Everyone has the power to influence the values of companies by making their voices heard on twitter (no spamming though)

Stocks are cheap enough that anyone can afford them and change in price quickly enough that everyone can make their fortune in minutes and move on to more important things

There are no losers. Only winners and bigger winners

Economics is simple enough that it doesn't take a PhD to understand. Stock trading systems should reflect this
